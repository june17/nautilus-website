ABOUT

NAUTILUS 2K13 , FISAT's National level tech fest returns this year to "Paint The Town BLUE". With 2013 being declared the UN Year of Water Cooperation, NAUTILUS follows suit with the theme "Planet Blue", focussing on water conservation.We've all sorts of events this year ranging from Auto show to GDG's CODELAB to our showcase event Poseiduos. Join us on the 6th and 7th of September and prepare to be washed away !
